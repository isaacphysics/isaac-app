This area of our platform aims to provide an opportunity to practise the material that features directly in school exams.

How to use this area of Isaac?

Our intention is that having worked through the problems from the Machacek and Crowter book, you come to this part of Isaac to check your answers.

  1. Clicking on one of the chapter links below will take you to a hexagon board with each hexagon representing a page of the book.
  2. When you reach this board click on the hexagon corresponding to the page of questions you want to check.
  3. The final page will present you with the questions and answer boxes to enter you your answers and check them.
